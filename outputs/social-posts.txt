【标题】
I built a free tool that scores trending crypto coins across 5 signals (trending, price, momentum, volume, community) — updated hourly

【正文】
Been working on this side project for a while and finally want to share it with the community.

It's called CryptoRadar (cryptoradar.dev) — a real-time ranking dashboard that scores the top trending coins from CoinGecko across 5 weighted signals:

T — Trending (20%) — CoinGecko search ranking
P — Price action (15%) — 24h price change
M — Momentum (20%) — volatility expansion from 7-day sparklines
V — Volume surge (25%) — volume-to-market-cap ratio
C — Community activity (20%) — GitHub commits, Twitter, Telegram, Reddit mentions

Everything normalizes into a 0–100 signal score, updated every hour automatically.

Here's what I've been adding recently:
• Token detail pages with score breakdown, price chart, and CoinGecko description
• Trending page showing which coins' scores are rising the fastest
• Most volatile page sorted by price momentum
• Full methodology page explaining how each signal works

It's completely free, no ads, no signup. Just wanted to build something useful for tracking the space.

Would love to hear what you think — what signals would you add or change?

www.cryptoradar.dev
